  • In-vehicle context-aware information filtering system(ICIFS)

    Solve the problem of
    information overload
    during driving and
    improve driver concentration

Why ICIFS important?

While the in-vehicle system is becoming intelligent, it may increase the probability of driver distraction.
ICIFS can reduce driver distraction.
Cooperate with infrastructure equipment to filter and provide the most important information based on AI to reduce information overload.

Background

Although autonomous driving is very popular
the current mainstream is still driven by human drivers.

In the context of a weak global economy
it is unlikely that L5 level autonomous driving will be
fully realized in a short period of time
and autonomous driving poses considerable safety risks.
